Our Story

Coffee as craft.
Community as calling.

Hearthstone started with a secondhand La Marzocco and a conviction that great coffee changes a morning — and a neighbourhood.

We source directly from small farms across Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala, roasting in-house every Thursday so every cup reaches you at its peak.
